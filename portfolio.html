Grumble Stylescape

This is a brand stylescape designed for the start-up 'Grumble', which is a social application providing food recommendations to users based on their friends' eating choices and restaurants they visit. This stylescape depicts a fun, easy-going and an approachable company environment due to its bright color scheme and rounded, easy-to-read typography scheme. This work was done working at the Entrepreneurship Hatchery at the University of Toronto.
