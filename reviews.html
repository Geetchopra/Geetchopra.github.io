GAME REVIEWS

As you may have realized, I love to play games! Through this page, I want to share my impressions of some games I have played over the years through a simple score table. I like the review scale that IGN uses and I apply a similar approach when I score games. Feel free to connect with me on social media if you want me to share more insights on how I calculated a particular score! I also hope to provide full reviews to some of these games in the future.

Filter anything here or click on the column names to sort the table.
TABLE LEGEND
GAME - The name of the game. I played all of these games for a significant amount of time before calculating a score. If the games have an end, then I have reached it. Some games get updated frequently and change a lot over the course of time. For this reason, if my review of a game also changes over time, you might see two or more entries for it.
RELEASE YEAR - The year in which the game was released on any platform.
PLATFORM - The platform that I played the game on. A game may be available on other platforms too.
GENRE - Primary genres of the game.
SCORE - My score (out of 10) of the game, based on multiple factors, like Story, Level Design, Gameplay and more.
