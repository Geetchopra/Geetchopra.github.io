CHESTNOTES MARKETING PORTFOLIO

PROJECT TIMELINE - September 2015 to Present
ROLES - Graphic Designer, Secretary
SKILLS INCORPORATED - Graphic Design, Leadership
SOFTWARE USED - Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Chestnotes is the A Capella choir, based at the Chestnut Residence at the University of Toronto. It was founded in 2015, when I was in my first year of college, and I have been a part of it ever since. I have also been responsible for all graphic design needs of the choir since the beginning. Since 2017, I have also taken up leadership roles as I gained seniority in the choir.

The marketing portfolio for Chestnotes below was designed entirely by me, with additional feedback from the choir director. Since there isn't any official branding for the choir, I experiment a lot with different design styles for promoting our events each year, which explains the significant difference in the poster designs each year.

All headings include a poster advertisement for our bi-annual concert and a program brochure, handed out during the event that highlights the songs performed by us.
