Influence people, build your town, customize your looks and more!

Tales of Harmonia is a casual arcade game which delivers a thoughtful message regarding the importance of community, togetherness and harmony through its engaging gameplay. You are in charge of the town of Harmonia, which has recently been destroyed by a devastating calamity. You have to rebuild the town of Harmonia in order to save its residents and preserve its legacy, but it cannot be done without the help of everyone in the community.

Tales of Harmonia was designed to spread some awareness, joy and hope, especially in the situation we currently are today, while providing a fun, casual pass-time for people of all ages!

You can enjoy lots of content, including the use of power ups, customizable looks, multiples modes of gameplay and more, as shown below.

Tales of Harmonia was designed and developed in Unity entirely by me, Geet Chopra. The idea stemmed from a simple game jam game I made which had the same premise. I felt that the game was a well balanced mix of simplicity and fun, so I decided to polish it and develop it for production on mobile! I thank everyone who decided to try out the game for their support!

I welcome all forms of feedback and suggestions regarding the game! You can use the contact form to submit any game related query and I will be in touch with you.
